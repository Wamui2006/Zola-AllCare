About Zola AllCare

We design psychosocial and emotional care systems that help people, teams, and communities thrive — sustainably.

Our Mission

Zola (“peace”) reflects our belief that care should be accessible, practical, and enduring. We exist to build safe spaces, trusted guidance, and strong peer networks so that support doesn’t end after a single session — it multiplies.

  • Evidence-informed programs delivered by certified professionals
  • Community-first design that outlives one-off interventions
  • Ethical practice, privacy, and trauma-informed facilitation

Vision & Values

Vision: A region where psychosocial care is a normal part of daily life, not a last resort.

Values: Interconnect · Compassion · Humanity · Dignity · Practicality

How We Work

Assess & Design

We begin with a needs assessment to understand context, risks, and strengths. Then we co-design a practical roadmap with clear outcomes.

Deliver & Equip

Care is delivered through guided conversations, safe spaces, and community circles — while building skills that people can reuse.

Integrate & Sustain

We embed routines, peer structures, and referral pathways so support continues — with or without us in the room.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Zola a clinic or a community program?

Zola is a care system. We deliver clinical and mentorship services (Z-Talk), safe spaces for institutions (Z-Space), and community circles (Z-Community), all routed through one team.

How do bookings work?

All bookings go through our single contact page. We match you to a clinician, mentor, or facilitator based on your needs.

Do you work outside Kajiado?

Yes. We run virtual sessions and travel for onsite programs, retreats, and community gatherings.

Ready to work with us? Tell us what you need — we’ll map the best next step.
